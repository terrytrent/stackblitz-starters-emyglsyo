Community Involvement
Our Impact
Read & Rise: Youth Literacy Outreach Partnering with local elementary schools, this program distributes free books and hosts volunteer reading sessions to support early literacy development.
Pens & Pages: Writing Mentorship for Teens In collaboration with high school English departments, Book Haven pairs aspiring teen writers with local authors and editors for monthly writing workshops and feedback sessions.
Books for All: Donation Drive An annual campaign to collect gently used books and school supplies for under-resourced classrooms, libraries, and community centers.
Events and Collaboration
Poetry & Pastries Night August 8, 2025 A cozy open-mic poetry event held quarterly in partnership with a local cafe, inviting the community to share original works over warm drinks and baked goods.
Blind Date with a Book Night September 11, 2025 Guests select a mystery-wrapped book labeled only with a few intriguing clues. A fun way to discover new genres and authors while mingling with fellow book lovers over refreshments.
Fall Author Fest: Voices of the Valley October 10, 2025 A seasonal event featuring readings and signings by regional authors, celebrating the voices that shape the local literary scene.
